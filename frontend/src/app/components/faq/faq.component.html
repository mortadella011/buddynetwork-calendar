Frequently Asked Questions

TL;DR

The calendar can be imported on any device/app that supports import from an iCal URL..
Just copy the link to your clipboard via "Copy Link" and proceed with your favourite app.

Import Calendar on iOS
  1. Copy calendar URL to clipboard and open Calendar app.
  2. Tap "Calendars"
  3. Tap "Add Calendar"
  4. Tap "Add Subscription Calendar"
  5. Paste URL in filed and tap "Subscribe"
  6. Tap "Add" to confirm the subscription
Import Calendar on Android
  1. Open the calendar app of your Google account on a desktop machine (for your convenience)
  2. Go to "Calendar"
  3. Right click on "My Calendars", go to "Add Calendar" and select "From Internet..."
  4. Paste URL and proceed with "OK"
  5. Confirm import and subscription to automatic updates with "Yes"
Import Calendar in Outlook for Microsoft 365 (Desktop)
  1. Copy calendar URL to clipboard and open Outlook.
  2. Click the plus sign next to "Other calendars"
  3. Click on "From URL"
  4. Paste URL and proceed with "Add calendar"
